About US

Welcome to GA Business Group

GA Business Group is a boutique Financial and Management consulting firm based in Vancouver, BC, Canada. GA Business Group was incorporated in 2007 and has been providing bookkeeping, accounting, controllership, financial and strategic management, MD & A reporting, M&A advising, corporate development, and project evaluations to public and private companies.

We believe our clients are of the utmost importance. We are committed to providing superior and reliable financial and management serives to help our clients focus on their goals and ensure their businesses going in the right direction and running efficiently.

Founder and Principle



Jenny Fang B Eng (GEOE), CPA, CMA

Ms. Fang is a Certified Management Accountant and a china registered geological engineer. Since 2005, Ms. Fang has been providing bookkeeping , accounting, financial reporting, controllership services to dozens of private and public companies. Ms Fang is the first place winner of "2013 Humorous Speech Contest " held by Toastmaster International District 21 Area B20. Ms. Fang held a Bachelor Degree of Geological Engineering from Central South University of China (1987), the top mining university in China. She worked as a geological engineer for a state-owned mining company for 12 years in China. With multitude of experience in accounting, financial reporting, geology, and linguistics (English & Mandarin), Ms. Fang has a great understanding of Mining and Natural Resources sector.
